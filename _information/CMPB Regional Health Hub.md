<h2><strong>Complimentary Shuttle Bus (Temporary)</strong></h2>
<p>The linkbridge from Cashew MRT and the pedestrian access from Rail Corridor
will open in Aug 2025. The complimentary shuttle service will operate from
23 Jun 2025 until the opening of the linkbridge to Cashew MRT in Aug 2025.&nbsp;
&nbsp;</p>
<p>Please use the complimentary shuttle bus service from the pick-up/drop-off
point outside Exit A of Cashew MRT station to get to CMPB.&nbsp; &nbsp;</p>
<p>The journey will take approximately 10 minutes, subject to traffic conditions.
The operational hours for the shuttle bus service are:&nbsp; &nbsp;</p>
<p>Monday to Friday: 7am to 7.30pm (15-minute intervals)&nbsp; &nbsp;</p>
<p>Saturday: 7am to 2pm (15-minute intervals)&nbsp; &nbsp;</p>
<p>*Please note that the shuttle bus is a temporary interim transportation
option and will cease when the linkbridge opens in Aug 2025.&nbsp;
